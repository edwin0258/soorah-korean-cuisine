This place is amazing!! My husband and I always stop in Medford as a halfway point from San Francisco to Portland. Last year we stumbled across this place and we were pleasantly surprised. The food here is very authentic and the owners and staff a really nice and helpful. If you come here for dinner do order the Seafood Jeon (seafood Korean pancake) along with the house made kimchi, which is made fresh by the owners wife. The Mushroom Tofu soup and Beef Tofu soup are also must try's, washed down with a Bohae raspberry wine or Kirin beer.
